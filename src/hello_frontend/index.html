About SpaceX

Learn more about SpaceX and our mission to revolutionize space travel.

Our Vision

Founded by Elon Musk, SpaceX is a private aerospace manufacturer and space transportation company. We design, manufacture, and launch advanced rockets and spacecraft with the goal of reducing space transportation costs and enabling the colonization of Mars.

Our Missions

Starship

Starship is a fully reusable spacecraft designed for interplanetary missions and colonization of Mars.

Falcon 9

The Falcon 9 is a two-stage rocket designed and manufactured by SpaceX for the reliable and safe transport of satellites.

Dragon

The Dragon spacecraft is designed to carry cargo, crew, and even astronauts to the International Space Station (ISS).
